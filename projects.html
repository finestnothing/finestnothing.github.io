Using AI to Predict Business Review Ratings on Yelp

Designed and trained ML model on dataset of Yelp business reviews to predict star rating of businesses based on text.

Fall 2021

Data was cleaned and prepared for training by creating a TFIDF Vectorizer which took the top 600 words between 1 and 2000 occurances. This was used to remove very commong words (such as "the", "and", "of", etc.) and focus on the more important words.

100,000 reviews were used for training and testing the model, using a 75/25 train/test split.

A Fully Connected Neural Network (FCN) model was built layer by layer. Approximately 20 variations were tested and compared, with the number of layers, nodes, and activation functions varied. These were then compared based on their RMSE score.

Predicted vs Real star ratings was plotted using matplotlib.
